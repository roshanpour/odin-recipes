Steak Marinade Recipe

Who doesn't love a good steak? Moreover, who doesn't love a good marinade? This strak marinade will have you drooling from start to finish.

This is a soy sauce based marinade that will make your steak delicipus. The ingredients used are fairly typical, so feel free to use your own creative spin. It's your kitchen after all!

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 1/3 cup fresh lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons garlic powder
  • 3 tablespoons dried basil
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons dried parsley flakes
  • 1 teaspoon ground white pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon hot pepper sauce
  • 1 teaspoon dried minced garlic

Steps

  1. Place the soy sauce, olive oil, lemon juice, Worcestershire sauce, garlic powder, basil, parsley, and pepper in a blender. Add hot pepper sauce and garlic, if desired. Blend on high speed for 30 seconds until thoroughly mixed.
  2. Pour marinade over desired type of meat. Cover, and refrigerate for up to 8 hours. Cook meat as desired.
