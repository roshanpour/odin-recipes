Sushi Recipe

This is a recipe of a sushi dish that can easily be made at home. Chefs spend years learning how to make sushi, but this dish should do the trick as your introduction. I love sushi, and I hope you'll love this meal as well.

You will want to use sushi grade fish, as that is the most important ingridient when it comes to sushi. There are no guarantees that sushi will turn out as good when you make it at home, so you have to put a good deal of effot into it. This delicious recipe is perfect for the occasion.

Ingredients

  • Nori
  • Sushi Rice
  • 1/2 lb. sushi-grade raw salmon
  • 4 oz cream cheese
  • 1 avocado
  • soy sauce

Steps

  1. Place the seaweed on a bamboo mat, then cover the sheet of seaweed with an even layer of prepared sushi rice. Smooth gently with the rice paddle.
  2. Layer salmon, cream cheese, and avocado on the rice, and roll it up tightly. Slice with a sharp knife, and enjoy with soy sauce.
